Goals

Pre Adoption Phase

In the Pre-Adoption phase, VerveIN focuses on foundational efforts. This includes platform development, strategic advertising, and building relationships with VR tech manufacturers and educational institutions. The goal is to create awareness, distinguish the platform, and establish a community presence. Clear goal-setting, user feedback collection, and investment in quality content drive progress, positioning VerveIN as a leading educational technology solution.

Post Adoption Phase

In the post-adoption phase, our primary goal is to expand our VR-based education services throughout the entire country. This strategic focus involves scaling the platform, enhancing its features, fostering a vibrant community, increasing presence in the education sector, optimizing cost-efficiency, and promoting innovation. Our overarching vision is to make immersive education accessible and transformative for a diverse range of educational institutions across the nation.
